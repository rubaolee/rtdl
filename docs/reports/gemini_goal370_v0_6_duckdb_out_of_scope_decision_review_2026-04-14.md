## Review of Goal 370 Documents

The review of `docs/goal_370_v0_6_duckdb_out_of_scope_decision.md`, `docs/reports/goal370_v0_6_duckdb_out_of_scope_decision_2026-04-14.md`, `docs/reports/gemini_v0_5_plus_v0_6_detailed_code_and_doc_audit_2026-04-13.md`, `docs/reports/goal347_v0_6_postgresql_graph_baseline_plan_2026-04-13.md`, and `docs/reports/goal369_v0_6_cit_patents_bfs_bounded_linux_eval_2026-04-13.md` confirms the following:

*   **DuckDB recommendation resolved honestly by explicit scope choice:** The documents clearly address the audit's recommendation for DuckDB. Both `docs/goal_370_v0_6_duckdb_out_of_scope_decision.md` and `docs/reports/goal370_v0_6_duckdb_out_of_scope_decision_2026-04-14.md` explicitly state that DuckDB is considered and then intentionally not adopted, and is out of scope for the current `v0.6` line. This resolves the audit item by a clear scope choice rather than an oversight.

*   **PostgreSQL remains the coherent chosen SQL baseline:** The plan in `docs/goal_370_v0_6_duckdb_out_of_scope_decision.md` identifies PostgreSQL as the SQL/database baseline for `v0.6`. `docs/reports/goal347_v0_6_postgresql_graph_baseline_plan_2026-04-13.md` further details PostgreSQL's role as a bounded external correctness and performance baseline for `bfs` and `triangle_count`. `docs/reports/goal369_v0_6_cit_patents_bfs_bounded_linux_eval_2026-04-13.md` demonstrates its active use and successful parity in evaluations, confirming its status as the chosen SQL baseline.

*   **Decision language does not overclaim:** Across the reports, particularly in `docs/reports/goal370_v0_6_duckdb_out_of_scope_decision_2026-04-14.md` and `docs/reports/goal347_v0_6_postgresql_graph_baseline_plan_2026-04-13.md`, there are explicit "Boundary" or "What PostgreSQL should not be claimed to be" sections. These sections clearly delineate the scope and limitations of the decisions and evaluations, ensuring that no overclaims are made regarding the capabilities of PostgreSQL or the general suitability of DuckDB. The language used is precise and avoids broad generalizations.
