## Surviving roadmap position

The final roadmap that survives criticism is:

- one primary user:
  - advanced technical users who want to express one non-graphical RT kernel
    without first hand-writing separate backend-specific implementations
- one release-defining bet:
  - **new-workload-first**
- one constrained secondary bet:
  - generate-only mode for one accepted kernel family only
- one supporting track:
  - OptiX/Embree performance maturation on real accepted hardware

## Final position

Goal 107 is accepted.

RTDL v0.2 is a promising target only under the revised, stricter roadmap:

- workload-first
- codegen constrained and killable
- hardware claims kept honest
- scope expansion forced through a charter before implementation
